About Us

Ya Ya Kitchen‎ offers delicious dining, carryout and delivery to Perrysburg, OH.

Ya Ya Kitchen‎ is a cornerstone in the Perrysburg community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
